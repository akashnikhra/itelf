Information Technology Entrepreneurship & Leadership Forum, fondly known as iTELF, is the Entrepreneurship Cell of SCIT which provides a dais for upcoming IT Entrepreneurs and Leaders to explore the world of start-ups, exchange ideas, foster and enhance their leadership skills.
This forum helps the entrepreneurs of today to metamorphose into victorious leaders of tomorrow. iTELF in it’s spirit believes that an entrepreneur is someone who creates, takes risks and lives his passion.

SproutIT is a national level event organised by iTELF, the Entrepreneurship Cell of SCIT, in which students and executives from all over the country take part in various events organised by the students of SCIT that gives them a platform to exhibit their managerial and technical skills. It is held with a lot of enthusiasm and fervour, and is a big hit every year.
This year Symbiosis Centre for Information Technology is back with SproutIT’16 which is scheduled to be held on 17 th September,2016 at Symbiosis Infotech Campus, Hinjawadi, Pune. SproutIT presents ZEIT ( Zeal of Entrepreneur to Innovate and Transform ) - a plethora of exciting events which include our Flagship Event-Marvel and Startup Event-Avviare. We invite you to be a part of this spectacular experience and make it a grand success!!
