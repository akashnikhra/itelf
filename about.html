Zeal of Entrepreneur to Innovate and Transfrom

A true entrepreneur is someone who, in addition to having business ideas and management adeptness, understands the significance of time to reach new heights of success. Taking that into account, SCIT this year emphasises on the importance of ‘time’ and brings to you ‘ZEIT-Zeal of Entrepreneur to Innovate and Transform’.

The soul of ZEIT lies in the belief that ‘time’ is the core essence of any business, and in this era of start-ups, it is undoubtedly the most important parameter leaders attach weight to. Without knowing the value of ‘time’, no amount of adroitness or cognizance would make a business successful. Thus at ZEIT we have sundry events revolving around ‘time’ as its central idea. We look forward to the participation of students and professionals alike, and understand their standpoint on this concept.

“Time is really the only capital that any human being has, and the only thing he can’t afford to lose.” by “-Thomas Edison”
