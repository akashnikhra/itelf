Registration

Event Description:

It is a Leadership/team building event in which the participants will be tested on their leadership and team building skills.Here the leadership and team building spirit of the individuals will be tested.

Special Suits:

  • Registration Fee: ₹ 200 Per Team
  • Team Size: 4

How To Register:

  • Register Here
  • Along with registration, entries must also be mailed to us at itelf@scit.edu
  • In your entries, you need to mention your Name, Mobile Numbers, E-Mail Address, and Organization/College/University.
  • The subject of the mail should only be as per the below format specified:
    "Zavio_Leadership_TeamName_College/Organization/University_Name".
  • Participants are required to carry their registration slips along with them on the day of the event. Entries will not be entertained in case of any loss or destruction of the slip. You will have to register again to take part in that case.
  • College ID's should be carried along.

Rules of Event:

  • A maximum of 4 persons per team will be allowed.
  • A Team can be formed from different Organization/ College/ University.
  • Any damage or exploitation of any clue of team will lead to disqualification.
  • Points will be awarded on the basis of the time duration in which team completes the round.
  • Teams will be judged based on the cumulative points earned in different rounds.
  • The team not completing within stipulated time will not be eliminated however their scores will be calculated accordingly.
  • Spot registration is not permitted on the day of event.
  • No late entries will be entertained.
  • Required resources will be provided by the event team.
  • Rules are subjected at the discretion of the organizing committee.

Other Details:

  • Prizes Worth : ₹ 1500/-
  • Event Date: 17th September 2016.
  • Reporting time: 2:00 PM
