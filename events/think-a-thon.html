Think-A-Thon

Let’s use that head of yours.
Registration

Event Description:

So you think you know it all? Come and showcase your B- quiz knowledge.Think-A-Thon is all here to give you this opportunity.

Special Suits:

  • Team Size: 2 Participants per team
  • Entry Fee : ₹ 100/- Per Team

How To Register??

  • Register yourself Here
  • Along with registration, entries must also be mailed to us at itelf@scit.edu
  • In your entries, you need to mention your names, mobile numbers, e-mail address and Organization/ College/ University’s name.
  • The subject of the mail should only be as per the below format specified: "Thinkathon_ParticipantName_College/Organization/University_Name".
  • Participants are required to carry their registration slips along with them on the day of the event. Entries will not be entertained in case of any loss or destruction of the slip. You will have to register again to take part in that case.

Rules and Regulations:

  • No books or paper other than that provided by the Organizing Committee will be allowed.
  • No conferring will be allowed between teams during the Quiz.
  • Spot registrations are permitted on the day of event.
  • Any team found using smart phones or gadgets will be disqualified on the spot.
  • Participants are required to bring their College ID Cards.
  • Cross college teams are allowed.
  • Tie breaker will be conducted in case of any tie in any of the rounds.
  • Question with time limit must be completed within the time slot.
  • In case of discrepancies or any case not covered in these rules, the decision of quiz master would be considered final.
  • Each team participating will have to abide by the above mentioned rules.
  • Final decision of the Judges will be binding in case of any dispute.

Other Details:

  • Prize Money: ₹ 1500/ Worth
  • Event Date: 17th September 2016
  • Reporting Time: 12 : 30 P.M.
