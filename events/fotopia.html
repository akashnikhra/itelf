FOTOPIA

Registration

Events Description:

A picture can speak more than words. Ignite your figment of imagination and depict your thoughts through your lens.

Special Suits:

  • Team Size: 1
  • Window of the event : 6th September, 2016 12:00 am to 15th September, 2016 11:59 pm
  • Entries received after the mentioned time frame will not be entertained at any circumstance.
  • Entry Fee : FREE

Rules of Event:

  • Each participant can submit up to 3 images.
  • The images must be based on one of the following themes:
    • Street Photography
    • Food
    • Festivals
  • Also the participant must submit a 2 to 3 line description for each image.
  • Photographs must be in digital format. Only online entries will be eligible.
  • Only minor burning, dodging and/or color correction is acceptable, as is cropping.
  • High dynamic range images (HDR), collage and stitched panoramas are NOT acceptable.
  • The picture should be a single picture. It cannot be a collage and should be in JPEG format.
  • Photographs will be accepted as long as they do not constitute copyright infringement or fraud.
  • The photograph, in its entirety, must be a single work of original material taken by the contest participant. By registering to the contest, participant represents, acknowledges and warrants that the submitted photograph is an original work created solely by the entrant, that the photograph does not infringe on the copyrights, trademarks, moral rights, rights of privacy/publicity or intellectual property rights of any person or entity, and that no other party has any right, title, claim or interest in the photograph.
  • The photograph must not, in the sole and unfettered discretion of the Sponsor, contain obscene, provocative, defamatory, or otherwise objectionable or inappropriate content.

Submission Guidelines:

Step 1:

  • Register yourself at Here
  • Along with registration, entries must also be mailed to us at itelf@scit.edu.
  • Photograph should be mailed to us.
    • The subject of the mail and the file name should be only in format specified: "Fotopia_CollegeName_ContestantName_ThemeName".
    • Send us an email at itelf@scit.edu with the following information: contestant name, college name, mobile number, e-mail address, your phrase and the corresponding picture.

Step 2:

  • The photographs would be uploaded by organizing team on Sprout It’16 Facebook page by 11:59 pm on 15th September, 2016. You can search your photograph on the Sprout IT’16 facebook page and share it.
    • Sharing must be done with the following hash tags “#SCIT #SproutIT’16 #Fotopia #ContestantName”
    • Participants can share the link further on FB page.
  • Use of BOTS/ Fake Accounts to get Facebook likes will lead to disqualification.
  • Entries must be authentic.

Judging:

  • Scores will be calculated based upon:
    • 30 % -No of likes/Comments/Share on Facebook SproutIT’16 Page.
    • 70 % - Assessment by expert panel on the following parameters:
      • Originality and Creativity
      • Image Quality and visibility
      • Relevance to selected theme
      • Lighting and Framing
  • Winners will be notified by e-mail. Decisions will be final and binding.

Other Details:

  • Prizes Worth: ₹ 1500/-
